# Sungrow

Clean Power for All

Connect your Sungrow Inverter to Homey and get detailed insights into your solar energy production and battery usage.
Monitor your current power output, as well as your daily, monthly, yearly, and total generation. With the Sungrow app for Homey, you can use this data to optimize your energy usage, create Flows, and keep track of your system's performance — all in one place.
